Palladium Properties

Palladium is a rare, lustrous silvery-white metal discovered in 1803. It belongs to the platinum group metals and shares many of their exceptional properties.

  • Exceptional Catalyst: Widely used in catalytic converters for automobiles
  • Industrial Applications: Essential in electronics, dentistry, and chemical processes
  • Jewelry: Popular for its purity, durability, and hypoallergenic properties
  • Investment: Highly sought-after as a store of value and portfolio diversifier
